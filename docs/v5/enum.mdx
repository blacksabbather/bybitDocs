### category
_Unified Account_
* `spot`
* `linear`&emsp;USDT and USDC perpetual
* `inverse`&emsp;Inverse contract, including Inverse perp, Inverse futures
* `option`

_Normal Account_
* `linear`&emsp;USDT perp
* `inverse`&emsp;Inverse contract, including Inverse perp, Inverse futures

### orderStatus
* `Created`&emsp;order has been accepted by the system but not yet put through the matching engine
* `New`&emsp;order has been placed successfully
* `Rejected`
* `PartiallyFilled`
* `PartiallyFilledCanceled`&emsp;spot has this order status only
* `Filled`
* `Cancelled`
* `Untriggered`
* `Triggered`
* `Deactivated`
* `Active`&emsp;order has been triggered and the new active order has been successfully placed. Is the final state of a successful conditional order

### tickDirection
* `PlusTick`&emsp;price rise
* `ZeroPlusTick`&emsp;trade occurs at the same price as the previous trade, which occurred at a price higher than that for the trade preceding it
* `MinusTick`&emsp;price drop
* `ZeroMinusTick`&emsp;trade occurs at the same price as the previous trade, which occurred at a price lower than that for the trade preceding it
